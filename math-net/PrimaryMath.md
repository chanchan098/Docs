# Number

## Least common multiple


### Euclid's algorithm

$$LCM(a,b) = \frac{|a \times b|}{GCF(a,b)}$$

```
    4   6
    
       __1__
     4 | 6 
       | 4
        ——— __2__
         2  | 4
              4
             ———
              0

    great common factor is 2

```

$GCF(4,6)=2$

$$LCM(4,6)=\frac{4 \times 6}{2}=12$$
